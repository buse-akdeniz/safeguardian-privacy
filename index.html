Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

SafeGuardian ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect (Topladığımız Bilgiler)

To provide our emergency SOS and family tracking services, we collect the following data:

  • Location Data: We collect precise real-time location information to allow your family members to see your position and to provide location details during an SOS alert.
  • Emergency Contacts: We access your selected contacts only to send alerts during an emergency.
  • Account Information: We collect your name and email to manage your family subscription and account security.

2. How We Use Your Information (Bilgileri Kullanım Şeklimiz)

We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate real-time family tracking services.
  • To trigger emergency SOS alerts to your designated contacts.
  • To manage paid family member subscriptions.
  • Zero-Data Sale Policy: We NEVER sell your personal or location data to third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

3. Data Security and Retention (Veri Güvenliği ve Saklama)

  • Encryption: All sensitive data, especially location history, is encrypted during transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.
  • Retention: We only keep your data as long as your account is active. You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time within the app settings.

4. User Rights (Kullanıcı Hakları)

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access the data we have about you.
  • The right to request the deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.
